Download and install the iDispenser APP on your smartphone or your caregiver's smartphone through the following methods:

## 2. Account Registration and Login

If you are using iDispenser for the first time, please create an account:

1. Open the iDispenser APP and click "Register"

   <img src="./assets/301.webp" alt="301" width="35%" />

2. Select your country or region

3. Check to agree to the privacy policy terms and conditions

4. Enter a valid email address or mobile phone number for verification

5. Get and enter the verification code

6. Create a password and log in

7. After logging in, follow the APP prompts to agree and grant the APP location and Bluetooth permissions for your device

   <img src="./assets/302.webp" alt="302" width="35%" />

## 3. Wi-Fi Connection Setup

**Device enters AP mode**

<img src="./assets/1.svg" alt="1"  width="40%"  />

1. In the device's home clock mode, press and hold the "+" button on the device for 3 seconds to enter Wi-Fi AP mode;
2. At this time, the speaker will broadcast "AP Mode" voice, and the LED light will start flashing;
3. If there is no operation within 3 minutes, the device will automatically exit AP mode and return to home clock mode;
4. Press and hold the "+" button on the device for 3 seconds again to exit AP mode.

**Note:** Bluetooth and location services need to be enabled to pair the pill dispenser with the mobile device.

---

## 4. Device Pairing

### 4.1 Complete pairing through Auto Scan

1. Open the iDispenser APP, click "Add Device" on the APP homepage
2. Wait for auto scan to find the pill dispenser in the "Discovering Devices" section, when the pill dispenser you want to connect appears, click "Add"
3. Enter the Wi-Fi network name and password, then click "Next"
4. Wait for the mobile device and pill dispenser to connect

<img src="./assets/305.webp" alt="305" width="100%" />

5. After successful Wi-Fi connection, the APP shows "added successfully", click "Done" to return. At this time, the device's display will show the "<img src="./assets/2.svg" alt="2"  width="3%" />" symbol.

<img src="./assets/306.webp" alt="306" width="45%" />

### 4.2 Troubleshooting Connection Failures

If Wi-Fi connection fails, please check the following aspects:

1. Is the Wi-Fi SSID and password correct?
2. Is your Wi-Fi 5GHz? Please set it to 2.4GHz.
3. Reset the device: Press and hold the "+" button on the device to re-enter AP mode, and reconnect Wi-Fi.

**Important Note:** Once the pill dispenser is connected to the mobile device, the time displayed on the dispenser will be synchronized with the time of the mobile device. If the mobile device moves to a different time zone, the time of the pill dispenser will not change unless re-paired.

## 5. APP Operation Guide

On the homepage, click the "TH017 WIFI Pill Dispenser" device to enter the device configuration page.

<img src="./assets/309.webp" alt="309" width="35%"  />

### 5.1 Setting Medication Schedule

<img src="./assets/307.webp" alt="307" width="100%" />

When operating the pill dispenser through the APP for the first time, you need to set the medication schedule:

1. Enter the Dashboard
2. Click "Setup"
3. Click "Daily Doses" to select the number of doses per day
4. Click "Save" to save the dose selection

**Note:** You can turn on "Unlock cycle length" to customize the daily cycle if the preset daily cycle does not meet your needs.

5. Click Alarm 1, 2, 3, etc. in sequence to set the alarm
6. Set the time for the selected alarm period, add notes if needed, click "Save" to complete saving one alarm

**Note:** Cannot set the same alarm time repeatedly.

7. After setting all alarms, a pop-up window will appear. Click "OK" in the pop-up window.

### 5.3 Medication Reminders

**When it's time to take medication:**

1. The next dose window will turn red

2. The alarm will sound (if the dispenser is not muted)

3. Click "Silence Alarm" and confirm to turn off the alarm sound

4. After taking the medication, the next dose window will return to blue and display the next dose in the schedule

5. The pill dispenser has a built-in sensor that can detect when medication is taken out of the medication box

   <img src="./assets/29@4x.webp" alt="29@4x" width="50%" />

**Taking Medication in Advance**

To take medication before the scheduled time:
1. Click "Take in advance"
2. A pop-up window will appear to confirm your selection, click "OK"
3. Just like at the scheduled time, the next dose window will turn red and the alarm will sound
4. Then you can take the medication normally

**Note:** If you try to take medication one day in advance, the "Take in advance" button will not work.

<img src="./assets/30@4x.webp" alt="30@4x" width="50%" />

### 5.5 Alarms Overview

The alarm interface allows you to edit existing alarms or set new medication schedules.

**Note:** If you set a new medication schedule, the pill dispenser will automatically rotate and reset back to the starting position.

**Edit Alarm:**

1. Click one of the alarm time periods
2. Adjust the time or edit the notes for that alarm
3. Click "Save" to confirm changes

<img src="./assets/211@4x.webp" alt="211@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.6 Settings Overview

The settings interface allows you to change the following options:

- **Time zone:** Change the time zone
- **24-hour clock:** Activate to display time in 24-hour format
- **Replenishment reminder:** Reminder for replenishment at the end of the cycle, can adjust the remaining dose threshold that triggers the replenishment reminder, adjustment range: 1~9 Doses
- **Volume:** Adjust between high, medium, low, and mute
- **Ringtone:** Choose between three alarm ringtones A, B, and C
- **Initial alarm duration:** Adjust the initial alarm duration, selection range from 5 minutes to 2 hours. (If medication is not taken after the initial alarm sounds, the timeout alarm will start)
- **Timeout alarm duration:** Adjust the timeout alarm duration, selection range from 4 minutes to 1 hour. (This will stop the alarm, but the dose will be retained until the next alarm. Please take it as soon as possible, otherwise it will be recorded as non-compliance with doctor's orders)
- **Reset Alarm:** Reset all alarms
- **Reset All:** Reset everything and start over

## 7. Operating the Pill Dispenser

### Basic Operation Steps

1. **Select daily dose ring:** Select the daily dose ring that meets your needs (please refer to the "Understanding Daily Dose Rings" section in the manual)

2. **Place dose ring and medication:**
   - Place the daily dose ring in the center of the internal medication box
   - Put medication into the medication box, corresponding to the taking time (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
   - Place according to the numbers (1, 2, 3, etc.) on the ring
   - Do not place any medication in the white part
   - When you see the white part approaching the medicine outlet, it's time to refill

3. **Install battery:** Please refer to the "Install Battery" section in the manual

4. **Unlock the cover:**
   - Use the key to unlock the cover (please refer to the "Locking and Unlocking the Cover" section)
   - Put the medication cup inside the main body

5. **Insert power cord**

6. **Connect to Wi-Fi network:** Use the iDispenser APP to connect (please refer to the "Wi-Fi Setup" section)

7. **Set time:** Please refer to the "Set Time" section in the manual

8. **Adjust settings:**
   - Adjust volume, date format, and time format from the iDispenser APP (please refer to the "Settings Overview" section)
   - Or adjust alarm volume, alarm type, and select 12 or 24-hour format on the dispenser (please refer to relevant sections in the manual)

9. **Set medication time:**
   - Set medication schedule from the iDispenser APP (please refer to the "Setting Medication Schedule" section)
   - Or set medication alarm time on the dispenser (please refer to the "Setting Alarms" section in the manual)

10. **Lock the cover:** Use the provided key to close and lock the cover

11. **Automatic medicine dispensing:** At the specified medication time, the alarm will sound, and the medication cup will automatically extend

12. **Stop the alarm:** When you take out the medication cup, the alarm should stop automatically

13. **Return the medication cup:** After taking the medication, return the medication cup to the outlet until you hear a "click" sound, indicating that the medication cup is in the correct position

14. **Place the device:** Place the pill dispenser on a flat surface

## 8. Care and Maintenance

- **Clean the exterior:** Wipe the exterior with a soft damp cloth. Do not use harsh detergents

- **Clean the internal medication box:**
  - If needed, remove the internal medication box
  - Gently rinse/wipe each medication compartment
  - Allow the container to dry completely before repositioning or refilling

- **Waterproof precautions:** Do not immerse the external pill dispenser in water or any other liquid

- **Keep clean:** Ensure the internal medication box is clean and dry before refilling
