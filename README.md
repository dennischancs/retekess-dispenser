## 1. APP Download and Installation

Download and install the iDispenser APP on your or your caregiver's smartphone via the following methods:

## 2. Account Registration

If you are using iDispenser for the first time, please create an account:

1. Open the iDispenser APP and click "Register"
2. Agree to the Privacy Policy terms and conditions
3. Enter a valid email address or phone number for verification
4. Obtain the verification code and enter it
5. Create a password and log in

---

## 3. Wi-Fi Connection Setup

### Device Entering AP Mode

<img src="./assets/1.svg" alt="1"  width="40%"  />

1. On the device's home clock mode, long press the device's "+" button for 3 seconds to enter Wi-Fi AP mode;
2. At this time, the speaker will broadcast "AP Mode" voice, and the LED light will start flashing;
3. If no operation within 3 minutes, the device will automatically exit AP mode and return to home clock mode;
4. Long press the device's "+" button for 3 seconds again to exit AP mode.

**Note:** Bluetooth and location services need to be enabled to pair the pill dispenser with the mobile device.

---

## 4. Device Pairing Methods

### Method 1: Auto Scan

1. Open the iDispenser APP and click "Add Device" on the APP homepage
2. Wait for auto scan to find the pill dispenser in the "Discovering Devices" section
3. When the pill dispenser to be connected appears, click "Go to Add"
4. Enter the Wi-Fi network name and password, then click "Next"
5. Wait for the mobile device and pill dispenser to connect

### Method 2: Add Manually

1. Open the iDispenser APP and click the "‚äï" button on the APP homepage to enter the "Add Device" page
2. Select Others ‚Üí Others(Wi-Fi)
3. Click the pill dispenser to be connected
4. Ensure the pill dispenser is powered and in AP mode, then click "Next"

<img src="./assets/5@4x.webp" alt="5" width="60%" />

5. Enter the Wi-Fi password (same as the mobile phone Wi-Fi password), click "Next"

<img src="./assets/6@4x.webp" alt="6"  width="25%" />

6. On the AP mode page, click "Next"

<img src="./assets/7@4x.webp" alt="7"  width="85%"  />

7. Click "Go to Connect" and select the matching Wi-Fi SSID

<img src="./assets/8@4x.webp" alt="8"  width="85%" />

8. Go to the phone's Wi-Fi settings and select "TH017-XXXX" from the list to connect the dispenser
9. Return to the APP and wait for the mobile device and pill dispenser to connect
10. After successful connection, click "Done"
11. After successful AP connection, click "Back" to return to the "Add Device" page

### Successful Connection Indicator

After successful Wi-Fi connection, the "<img src="./assets/2.svg" alt="2"  width="3%" />" symbol will be displayed on the device's display.

<img src="./assets/9@4x.webp" alt="9" width="85%" />

### Connection Failure Troubleshooting

If Wi-Fi connection fails, please check the following aspects:

1. Are the Wi-Fi SSID and password correct?
2. Is your Wi-Fi 5GHz? Please set it to 2.4GHz.
3. Reset the device: Long press the device's "+" button to re-enter AP mode and reconnect Wi-Fi.

**Important Note:** Once the pill dispenser is connected to the mobile device, the time displayed on the dispenser will be synchronized with the mobile device's time. If the mobile device moves to a different time zone, the pill dispenser's time will not change unless re-paired.

## 5. APP Operation Guide

<img src="./assets/21@4x.webp" alt="21@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.1 Setting Medication Schedule

When operating the pill dispenser through the APP for the first time, you need to set up a medication schedule:

1. On the Dashboard, click "Setup"
2. Click "Doses per day" to select the number of doses per day
3. Click "Save" to save the dose selection

**Note:** You can open "Unlock Cycle Length" to customize the daily cycle if the preset daily cycle does not meet your needs.

4. Select alarm time periods to set alarms
5. Set times for the selected alarm periods, add remarks if needed

**Note:** The same alarm time cannot be set repeatedly.

6. After setting all alarms, a popup will appear. Click "Okay" in the popup. The dispenser will automatically rotate the tray back to the starting position
7. Another popup will appear, indicating that your tray is ready. Click "Okay" in the popup

### 5.2 Dashboard Overview

#### Next Dose

<img src="./assets/22@4x.webp" alt="22@4x" width="15%" /> Displays the next dose time, dose number, total doses, remarks for the next dose, and the option to take medicine in advance

#### Day Cycle

<img src="./assets/23@4x.webp" alt="23@4x" width="15%" /> Displays the currently selected daily cycle

#### Active Slots

<img src="./assets/24@4x.webp" alt="24@4x" width="15%" /> Displays the number of medicine boxes in use (e.g., 21 active slots)

#### Ends

<img src="./assets/25@4x.webp" alt="25@4x" width="15%" /> Displays the date when the pill dispenser needs to be refilled

#### Doses Left

<img src="./assets/26@4x.webp" alt="26@4x" width="15%" /> Displays how many doses remain to be taken (e.g., 21 doses left)

#### Plug Icon

<img src="./assets/28.svg" alt="28" width="8%" /> Indicates that the pill dispenser is plugged into the power cord

**Note:** If the pill dispenser is unplugged, the plug icon will disappear from the dashboard.

### 5.3 Medication Reminder

When it's time to take medication:

1. The next dose window will turn red
2. The alarm will ring (if the dispenser is not muted)
3. Click "Mute this alarm" to turn off the alarm
4. After taking the medication, the next dose window will return to blue and display the scheduled next dose
5. The built-in sensor in the pill dispenser can sense when medication is poured out of the medicine box

#### Taking Medicine in Advance

To take medicine before the set time:
1. Click "Take in advance"
2. A popup will appear confirming your choice, click "Okay"
3. Just like the scheduled time, the next dose window will turn red and the alarm will ring
4. Then you can take the medicine normally

**Note:** If you try to take medicine a day in advance, the "Take in advance" button will not work.

<img src="./assets/29@4x.webp" alt="29@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.4 Activity Overview

The activity interface displays records of when users took their medication:

- **Green Symbol:** Took medicine on time
- **Yellow Symbol:** Took medicine late
- **Red Symbol:** Missed medication

You can click the remark icon next to the time to view remarks for that specific dose.

#### Determination of Medication Time

- **On Time:** Took medicine during the set alarm duration
- **Late:** Took medicine after the alarm duration but before the next alarm
- **Missed:** Never took medicine and the next alarm has sounded

The alarm duration is set to 30 minutes by default, but can be adjusted (see "Settings Overview" section).

**Note:** Records will start to be deleted after 14 days.

<img src="./assets/210@4x.webp" alt="210@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.5 Alarms Overview

The alarms interface allows you to edit set alarms or set a new medication schedule.

**Note:** If a new medication schedule is set, the pill dispenser will automatically rotate and reset to the starting position.

**Editing Alarms:**
1. Click one of the alarm time periods
2. Adjust the time or edit remarks for that alarm
3. Click "Save" to confirm changes

<img src="./assets/211@4x.webp" alt="211@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.6 Settings Overview

The settings interface allows you to change the following options:

- **Date Format:** Change the date display format

- **24 Hour Time:** Activate to display time in 24-hour format

- **Volume:** Adjust between high, medium, low, and mute

- **Ringtone:** Choose between 2 alarm tones

- **Alarm Duration:** Adjust alarm duration, selectable range from 5 minutes to 2 hours

- **Push Notifications:** Activate notifications to receive the following reminders:
  - Refill reminder
  - Medication preparation reminder
  - Taken medication reminder
  - Missed medication reminder
  
  These notifications will be received even when the APP is not open. You can activate all or part of the notifications.

- **Reset Alarms:** Reset all alarms

- **Reset All:** Reset everything and start over

<img src="./assets/212@4x.webp" alt="212@4x" width="25%" />

### 5.7 Options Overview

Click the options <img src="./assets/3.svg" alt="3" width="3%" /> icon in the upper right corner of the screen to enter the options interface.

In this interface you can:
- View device information
- View device network
- Change pill dispenser name
- Share pill dispenser information with other APP users
- Completely remove the device from the APP

#### Device Updates

The iDispenser APP will notify you when the pill dispenser needs updating:
1. When a popup appears, click "Update Now" to enter the device update page
2. Click "Update" to start the update

## 7. Operating the Pill Dispenser

### Basic Operating Steps

1. **Select Daily Dose Ring:** Select the daily dose ring that meets your needs (please refer to the "Understanding Daily Dose Rings" section in the manual)

2. **Place Dose Ring and Medication:**
   - Place the daily dose ring in the center of the internal medicine box
   - Put the medication into the medicine box corresponding to the dosing time (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
   - Place according to the numbers on the ring (1, 2, 3, etc.)
   - Do not place any medication in the white sections
   - When you see the white approaching the dispensing port, it's time to refill

3. **Install Batteries:** Please refer to the "Installing Batteries" section in the manual

4. **Unlock Lid:**
   - Use the key to unlock the lid (please refer to the "Locking and Unlocking the Lid" section)
   - Place the medicine cup inside the main body

5. **Plug in Power Cord**

6. **Connect Wi-Fi Network:** Use the iDispenser APP to connect (please refer to the "Wi-Fi Setup" section)

7. **Set Time:** Please refer to the "Setting Time" section in the manual

8. **Adjust Settings:**
   - Adjust volume, date format, and time format from the iDispenser APP (please refer to the "Settings Overview" section)
   - Or adjust alarm volume, alarm type on the dispenser, and select 12 or 24-hour format (please refer to the relevant sections in the manual)

9. **Set Medication Times:**
   - Set medication schedule from the iDispenser APP (please refer to the "Setting Medication Schedule" section)
   - Or set medication alarm times on the dispenser (please refer to the "Setting Alarms" section in the manual)

10. **Lock Lid:** Use the provided key to close and lock the lid

11. **Automatic Dispensing:** At the designated medication time, the alarm will sound and the medicine cup will automatically push out

12. **Stop Alarm:** When removing the medicine cup, the alarm should stop automatically

13. **Return Medicine Cup:** After taking the medicine, put the medicine cup back into the outlet until you hear a "click" sound, indicating the medicine cup is in the correct position

14. **Place Device:** Place the pill dispenser on a flat surface

## 8. Care and Maintenance

- **Clean Exterior:** Wipe the exterior with a soft damp cloth. Do not use abrasive cleaners

- **Clean Internal Medicine Box:**
  - If necessary, remove the internal medicine box
  - Gently rinse/wipe each medicine compartment
  - Allow the container to dry completely before repositioning or refilling

- **Waterproof Notice:** Do not immerse the external pill dispenser in water or any other liquid

- **Keep Clean:** Ensure the internal medicine box is clean and dry before refilling
